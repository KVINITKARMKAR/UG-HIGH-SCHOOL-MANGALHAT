About School

Upgraded Government High School, Mangalhat is a well-established government co-educational institution located in Mangalhat, Rajmahal Block of Sahibganj District, Jharkhand. The school has been serving the educational needs of the local community for several decades and is committed to providing quality and inclusive education to students from diverse backgrounds.

Established in 1969, the school has steadily grown in academic strength, infrastructure, and community trust. Over the years, it has evolved into an important center of learning, nurturing young minds with knowledge, discipline, and strong moral values. The institution functions under the guidelines of the Government of Jharkhand and follows the prescribed academic curriculum.

The school offers education from primary to secondary level, with a strong focus on the holistic development of students. Along with academic excellence, equal importance is given to character building, social responsibility, discipline, and extracurricular activities. A dedicated team of qualified and experienced teachers works continuously to create a safe, supportive, and engaging learning environment.

Upgraded Government High School, Mangalhat firmly believes that education is the foundation of a progressive society. With this vision, the school strives to empower students with the skills, confidence, and values necessary to succeed in higher education and future careers, while becoming responsible and ethical citizens of the nation.

Our Vision

To create a nurturing educational environment that promotes academic excellence, moral integrity, and lifelong learning, enabling students to become confident, responsible, and productive members of society.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education accessible to all students.
  • To foster intellectual growth, discipline, and ethical values.
  • To encourage critical thinking, creativity, and innovation.
  • To support the overall physical, emotional, and social development of students.
  • To prepare students for higher education and responsible citizenship.
